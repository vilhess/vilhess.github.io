About

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D at the LITIS Lab, INSA Rouen, with a research focus on time series anomaly detection, forecasting, and the development of foundation models for zero-shot forecasting.

I have a strong background in Mathematics, with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Paris-Saclay University and a Master's degree in Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence from the same institution.

PatchFM · Foundation Model

PatchFM — my patch-based foundation model for zero-shot time series forecasting with quantile predictions (model card). 

!pip install patchfm
from patchfm import Forecaster, PatchFMConfig
model = Forecaster(PatchFMConfig())  # pretrained, from HF Hub
seq = torch.randn(batch_size, context_size)
median, quantiles = model(seq, forecast_horizon=64)
